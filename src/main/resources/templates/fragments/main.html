Community

Corner Gate Christian Church has been up and running for 11 years and has played an integral role in the community. Faith and togetherness is the backbone of a strong cummunity and Corner Gate Church aims to exemplify that maxim through actions and open gates.

Trust

Corner Gate Christian Church deeply values the trust that their community has placed in their church. They understand that trust is something that is earned, and they work tirelessly to build and maintain it. From their welcoming atmosphere to their commitment to serving the needs of the community, Corner Gate Christian Church is dedicated to fostering a strong and positive relationship with those they serve. They believe that the trust of their community is a precious gift that they must cherish, and they strive to live up to the expectations of their congregants each and every day. Through their actions, they hope to continue to earn the trust and respect of those they serve, and to make a positive impact on the lives of those in their community.
